Gabriela Guazzo

Marketing Manager, Latin America

Gabriella Guazzo is the Marketing Manager for Hewlett Packard Enterprise in Latin America. She held the same role at HP, prior to the company’s separation into two companies –Hewlett Packard Enterprise and HP Inc. Gabriella join Hewlett Packard in 1991 as a services consultant in HP Venezuela, being mainly responsible to sell and implement solutions to customers throughout Latin America.

Gabriella has nearly 15 years of experience in marketing, communications and business management, with a demonstrated history of working in the information technology and services industry. Strong marketing professional skilled in Marketing Management, Product and Services Lifecycle Management, and People Management, Gabriella leads today a multi-culture team of marketing professionals that build pipeline and contribute to meaningful revenue for solutions and products in Latin America.

Gabriella has a bachelor’s degree in computer science from Universidad Central de Venezuela and a master’s degree in Business Administration from the University of Miami.

She is based in Miami, Florida and speaks fluent English, Spanish, Portuguese and Italian.

Marcelo Castro

Founder and CEO

As founder of MarketLogic, Marcelo brings over 20 years of international marketing experience at both strategic and tactical levels. Since venturing on his own, he has successfully created, developed and implemented partner marketing, digital and demand generation campaigns for a wide array of clients in the technology sector such as HPE, Adobe, Microsoft, VMware, Intel and Schneider Electric.

As president of MarketLogic, he is currently responsible for overseeing strategy and ongoing business development initiatives. Marcelo is also charged with creating a customer-oriented culture based on developing long-term relationships with new and existing stakeholders.

Prior to creating MarketLogic, he created XL Worldwide a logistics company specializing in courier services ranging from person to person delivery to the timely distribution of marketing materials throughout the Americas.

Marcelo Castro earned his MBA Degree from the University of Florida and is currently completing his Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA) from the University of Manchester (UK).

He resides in Pembrokes Pines, Florida with his wife and three children.
